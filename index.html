Frequently Asked Questions

What is Disney+?

+

Disney+ is the home of Disney, Pixar, Marvel, Star Wars, National Geographic, Star, and more.

What can I watch on Disney+?

+

Disney+ offers you a growing collection of stories:

  • Exclusive original series and movies
  • Timeless classics from Walt Disney Animation Studios
  • Entertainment stories including comedies, dramas, real-life inspired stories, etc.
  • National Geographic documentaries, special productions, and much more.

What is the price of a Disney+ subscription?

+

You can choose from three levels of Disney+ subscription:

  • Premium (€11.99/month or €119.99/year): Resolution up to 4K Ultra HD and HDR. 4 simultaneous streams. Downloads for offline viewing on up to 10 devices. Dolby Atmos audio on compatible content.
  • Standard (€8.99/month or €89.99/year): Resolution up to 1080p Full HD. 2 simultaneous streams. Downloads for offline viewing on up to 10 devices. 5.1 surround sound on compatible content.
  • Basic (€5.99/month): Resolution up to 1080p Full HD. 2 simultaneous streams. 5.1 surround sound on compatible content. Ad-supported content.

What devices and platforms can I use with Disney+?

+

Disney+ is accessible on mobile devices, web browsers, game consoles, TV boxes, and smart TVs.

Does a Disney+ subscription have a commitment period?

+

Disney+ has no commitment and you can cancel your subscription at any time. The cancellation will take effect at the end of the period you have paid for.

  1. Log in to www.DisneyPlus.com
  2. Select your profile
  3. Select Account
  4. Select your subscription
  5. Select Cancel Subscription
  6. Select Continue to cancel
